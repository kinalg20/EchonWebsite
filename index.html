    • About Us

    Echon has over 25 years of experience in PVC extrusion manufacturing and is a premier supplier of PVC products such as Sheets, Trimboards, Mouldings, Doors, Signage etc. worldwide. Besides PVC and other building products, the Echon group offers its customers an extensive range of stones – Marble, Granite, Sandstone, Quartz – under the brand name, Echon Realm (our stone segment).
    Echon Realm is a popular choice for numerous applications such as kitchen, vanity, furniture, bathroom, flooring, wall cladding etc. Echon Realm provides semi-finished products in the form of slabs/sheets. We also work with our customers to customize any design in Quartz and fabricate end-products from our stone segment.
